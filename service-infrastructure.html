What materials do you recommend for energy-efficient construction?

We recommend sustainable materials such as insulated concrete, recycled steel, energy-efficient glass, and eco-friendly wood. These materials improve thermal performance, reduce energy consumption, and support environmentally responsible construction.

Do you collaborate with contractors or handle construction management?

Yes, we collaborate closely with trusted contractors and project managers throughout the construction process. This coordination ensures design accuracy, efficient timelines, and smooth communication between all teams involved.

What is the difference between architecture and interior design?

Architecture focuses on the structural design, layout, and functionality of a building, while interior design focuses on interior spaces, materials, lighting, and aesthetics to enhance comfort and usability.

How do you collaborate with contractors during construction?

We maintain regular communication with contractors through site visits, design reviews, and progress meetings. This ensures the project follows the original design intent while maintaining quality and efficiency.
